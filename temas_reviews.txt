1. Host/Staff (H):
- H1: Hospitality & Warmth (friendliness, being welcoming)
- H2: Communication (responsiveness, speed)
- H3: Local Guidance (provided tips/recommendations)
- H4: Issue Resolution (problem-solving, fairness)
- H5: Language Skills (language barriers or communication ease)
2. Operations & Logistics (G):
- G1: Check-in/out Logistics (ease, timing, key exchange)
- G2: Cleaning Service (cleaning provided during stay)
- G3: Breakfast & Included Services (quality of food/drinks)
- G4: Extra Services (tours, transfers organized)
- G5: House Rules (rules fairness/impact)
3. Property Interior (P):
- P1: Maintenance Condition (repairs, age, renovations)
- P2: Initial Cleanliness (cleanliness upon arrival)
- P3: Comfort & Equipment (beds, WiFi, amenities)
- P4: Size & Spaciousness (perceived space)
- P5: Aesthetics & Design (decor, visual appeal)
- P6: Light & Views (natural light, views)
4. Layout & Experience (L):
- L1: Group Suitability (families/groups)
- L2: Long-stay Suitability (storage, laundry, kitchen)
- L3: Privacy (degree of privacy)
- L4: Accessibility (stairs, elevators)
- L5: Private Outdoor Spaces (balcony, patio, etc.)
- L6: Traveler Profile (nomads, couples, etc.)
- L7: Social Interaction (shared areas, socializing)
5. Building (B):
- B1: Building Security (doorman, gated access)
- B2: Acoustic Insulation (soundproofing, noise from neighbors)
- B3: Building Type (structure style)
- B4: Building Amenities (pool, gym, shared laundry)
- B5: Parking (availability, security)
6. Neighborhood/Location (N):
- N1: Strategic Location (central, convenient)
- N2: Connectivity (public transport, taxi/Uber ease)
- N3: Area Security (safety perception)
- N4: Walkability (sidewalks, parks nearby)
- N5: Nearby Services (stores, banks, pharmacy)
- N6: Leisure & Culture (restaurants, museums)
- N7: External Noise Environment (traffic, night noise)
- N8: Neighborhood Vibe (atmosphere, character)
- N9: International Friendliness (ease for non-locals)
7. Accuracy (A):
- A1: Listing Accuracy (photo/description match)
8. Value/Price (V):
- V1: Value for Money (perceived value)
9. General Satisfaction (S):
- S1: Global Satisfaction (overall judgment)
- S2: Recommendation/Return (willingness to recommend or return)
- S3: Repeat Guest (explicit repeat stay)
